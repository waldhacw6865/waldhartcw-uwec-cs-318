Why Exercise is Important

Exercise is essential for guinea pigs to maintain a healthy weight, prevent boredom, and promote overall well-being. Regular physical activity helps to strengthen their muscles, improve cardiovascular health, and keep them mentally stimulated.

Simple Exercise Ideas

Provide your guinea pig with a spacious enclosure that allows them to move freely. Add tunnels, ramps, and hideouts to encourage exploration. You can also create a safe play area outside their cage for supervised floor time.

Interactive Toys

Interactive toys, such as treat balls or chewable items, can keep your guinea pig engaged and active. Rotate their toys regularly to maintain their interest and prevent boredom.
