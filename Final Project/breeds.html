American Guinea Pig

The American guinea pig is one of the most popular breeds due to its friendly nature and easy care. They have short, smooth coats and come in a variety of colors and patterns. This breed is perfect for first-time guinea pig owners.

Abyssinian Guinea Pig

The Abyssinian guinea pig is known for its unique coat, which features rosettes (swirls of fur) all over its body. They are playful and curious, making them a favorite among guinea pig enthusiasts. Their coats require regular grooming to keep them looking their best.

Peruvian Guinea Pig

The Peruvian guinea pig is easily recognizable by its long, flowing hair. This breed requires more grooming than others but is loved for its striking appearance. They are gentle and make great companions for those willing to put in the extra effort.
